QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

Common Questions

Everything you need to know about giving your Zakat to KPMS.

Is my donation Zakat-eligible? +
Yes. KPMS serves students from families who qualify as Zakat recipients under Islamic guidelines. Your contribution funds education for children who would otherwise not have access, which is a recognized category of Zakat distribution. You can toggle the Zakat marker on your donation for your personal records.
Why Zelle instead of a regular donation platform? +
Platforms like GoFundMe charge 2.9% + $0.30 per transaction — on a $1,000 donation, that's $29.30 that never reaches the children. Zelle transfers are free for both sender and receiver, meaning 100% of your $1,000 goes directly to KPMS. When every dollar matters, we refuse to lose any to processing fees.
How will I know my donation was used properly? +
Every donor receives quarterly impact reports with photographs, student progress metrics, and detailed financial breakdowns showing exactly how funds were allocated. We believe in radical transparency — you deserve to see your Zakat at work.
Is my donation tax-deductible? +
Tax deductibility depends on your country's regulations. We provide detailed donation receipts that you can submit to your tax professional. For donors in the United States, we are working to establish 501(c)(3) status through a fiscal sponsor. Contact us for the most current information.
Can I donate monthly / set up recurring giving? +
Absolutely. You can set up a recurring Zelle transfer through your bank, or contact us to arrange a monthly commitment. Recurring donors receive priority updates and a special end-of-year impact summary.
